Founder

Aashna Khater

Aashna Khater is a First Year Engineering student currently pursuing her Coumputer Science degree from Pune Institute of Computer Technology in Pune, Maharastra. Aashna believes that the current generation has a huge responsibility to protect and hand on the planet to the next generation. She wants people to inculcate a habit to seek solutions to our day to day environment damaging lifestyle.

Inspiration for Clearthemess

"One day, I realised that my assumption about people being aware enough to avoid generating waste was wrong. I then decided to do something which would make people think about the consequences of their actions and inspire them to change their ways. For quite some time now I've been wondering about what I can do for our environment and our ignorance towards it. I decided to take tiny steps towards it and that's how clearthemess was born :)"
