Why use a Menstrual Cup?

As compared to tampons and sanitary pads:
- eco friendly alternative.
- relatively inexpensive.
- safe to use.
- reusable.
- lasts for 4-12 hours.
- don't interfere with vaginal flora, tampons do.
- can hold twice as much liquid as sanitary napkins and tampons.
- leakage is lower than disposable pads or tampons.
- odourless since no oxidation (blood doesn't come in contact with oxygen).
Isn't it worth a try? :) . . . . .

Tampons and sanitary pads generate carbon footprint of 5.3 kg CO2 equivalents and 200000 tonnes of waste per year. Each user throws 125 to150 kg waste of tampons, pads and applicators.

Feel free to comment your personal experience with the cup or any other alternatives you could suggest! . Let's help each other to overcome our fear of using a menstrual cup :D . Some alternatives I came across - reusable pads, reusable tampon applicators and period underwear.
