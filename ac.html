Air coolers or Air conditioners?

Benefits of air coolers over ACs: (Work best in hot and dry areas)
- eco friendly
- consumes 80-90% less electricity
- tremendous decrease in carbon emissions
- uses water as a refrigerant, ACs use CFCs
- Cheaper : For a 200 sq ft room,air cooler would cost about Rs 6000-8000 whereas an AC would cost about Rs 35000-40000.
- fresh air -7-8 health risks caused by ACs
