Eco-Friendly Pencils

Isn't it a brilliant idea to use newspaper instead of wood to make these eco-friendly pencils? And whats more, it comes with a seed capsule that can be planted after the pencil is used! They're 100% eco-friendly :). . . . . .

168 trees! Amazing. So I've been using these pencils for a while now and were one of the reasons behind coming up with this idea! Found the company's page @araatrika and got these mind blowing statistics! Thank you @araatrika for helping us make this world a little better :D. . . . . .
