Buckets over Showers?

Ahah obvious solution, right? But we know it's difficult to get out of our comfort zone :p . . . . .

According to the research, on an average a 10 minute shower requires 50 litres of water and a bucket of water requires 18 litres, time to make a choice :) . . . .

In my opinion, you can bathe for a longer time using a bucket than taking a shower and save more water! :) Let me know what y'all think about this! . . . .
